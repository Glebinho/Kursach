Real Madrid return to training tomorrow

Real Madrid are back in training tomorrow. The league leaders will train at Real Madrid City after the Christmas break and begin preparations for their next fixture: matchday 19 of the championship against Mallorca, which will be played on Wednesday 3 January at the Santiago Bernabéu stadium (7:15 pm CET). The session will kick off at 4pm and will be held behind closed doors.
